FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Got questions? We've got answers

What is Woostaa? +

Woostaa is a professional home services platform founded by Abdul Waris (CEO) and Aryan Raj (CFO) that connects you with verified, trained professionals for services like deep cleaning, pest control, maid services, cooking, driver services, and car washing in Bangalore. We simplify blue-collar hiring with quality, speed, and reliability.

How are professionals verified? +

Every professional undergoes comprehensive verification including government ID checks, police background verification, skill assessments, and in-person interviews before joining our platform. Our founders ensure each worker meets our high standards.

What areas do you serve in Bangalore? +

We currently serve major areas in Bangalore including Yelahanka, Koramangala, Whitefield, Electronic City, and select residential societies. We're expanding to cover more areas soon and planning expansion to Hyderabad, Pune, Mumbai, Delhi, and Noida.

How do I book a service? +

You can book services through WhatsApp by clicking the booking buttons on our website or using our chat assistant. Our team will help you schedule the service at your convenience with transparent pricing and no hidden costs.

Is there a quality guarantee? +

Yes! We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with the service, we'll send another professional to redo it at no extra cost. Quality is our top priority.
